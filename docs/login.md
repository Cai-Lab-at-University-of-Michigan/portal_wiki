## Login & Account Setup

Go to the login page:
{{PORTAL_URL}}/login

Three login options are supported:

### 1. Manual login
- Enter your `email` and `password` to sign in, or  
- Click `Sign Up` to create a new account

### 2. Sign in with Google
- Supports Google accounts, including most university-managed Google emails

### 3. Sign in with ORCID
- Supports login using ORCID credentials

---

## First-time Login

If this is your first time logging in, please contact the site administrator to activate your account.

---

### Authorization Notice
If you choose Google or ORCID login, you may be prompted to authenticate with those services and approve access.  
The system may collect basic information (e.g., email and username) to create and manage your account.
